About Us

The Drop Box is an online grocery shopping and delivery platform launched in 2020 and is based in Cape Town, South Africa.


This platform was launched to help and assist people during this global pandemic.


Our aim is to enable people to be able to purchase their essential goods while staying safe at the comfort of their home.


Our customers purchase their essentials from their choice of grocery store and we make sure that their goods safely delivered to them following our hygiene procedures.
